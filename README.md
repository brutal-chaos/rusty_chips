# rusty_chips

A chip8 emulator

## Controls

### Escape key to open the menu, controls are as follows:

```
|---------------|   |---------------|
| 1 | 2 | 3 | C |   | 1 | 2 | 3 | 4 |
| 4 | 5 | 6 | D | = | Q | W | E | R |
| 7 | 8 | 9 | E | = | A | S | D | F |
| A | 0 | B | F |   | Z | X | C | V |
|---------------|   |---------------|
```
